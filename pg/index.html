PROSPECTIVE PG

With an extraordinary group of teachers and peers, IIT Mandi offers one of India’s finest PG programs. With a vast pool of resources including a gigantic library and its number of labs , IIT Mandi has managed to create the perfect nest to design and build the next future.

Message from Dean (Students)

Student life is about learning: not only one's academic subjects and course material, but also learning to adapt and thrive in the world, make life-long friends, and acquire skills and values. The office of the Dean Students at IIT Mandi is engaged in helping students learn -- in every sense of the term -- to the best of their ability.... The IITs have a strong tradition of encouraging students to take an active part in the life of the community. Thus, students come to organize workshops, lectures, tutorials, extra-curricular events, leisure time activities, and notably major cultural and technical festivals. The undisputed success of these events in an index of the growing self-confidence and self-reliance with which students team go about organizing them. Imperceptibly, these engagements contribute to the professionalization of the prospective engineer. If one has to answer the question how engineers come to think like engineers, the answer unerringly points to such opportunities outside the class room to articulate one's ideas, to share common goals, and to pursue them with meticulous teamwork.

As a practical learner, the professors helped me familiarise myself with my studies and applying the class room based theoretical retrospective concepts into practical perspective. Their wisdom surprises me in every way and made me rethink about the society we live in and encourages me to contribute to it for our betterment.I am looking forward to gain more experiences ahead.The aforementioned statements are partially false I just do it for my gang

Shreya Sharma , IIT Mandi

IIT Mandi is a serene, research-friendly institute nestled away from the hustle and the bustle of city life. After completing my M.Sc. Mathematics from IIT Ropar with a certificate of merit, I used to think that joining IIT Mandi was the right decision or not. But after spending 6 months in this beautiful campus, my perception changed.
The faculty members here are extremely enthusiastic about both research and teaching. The central library, which has common discussion rooms and silent study rooms, is also a splendid area to study. I also worked as Coordinator SCRI and student member anti ragging committee.

Manoj Kumar , IIT Mandi

I am currently a PhD student at IIT Mandi, and I am nearing the end of my second year here. Students from the institute do have opportunities to involve in foreign exchange programmes and learn alongside other international programmes. It has been a rewarding experience to share and apply research findings with the IIT Mandi community. The research atmosphere here is extremely nice, and students and my batchmates are more focused on study and research owing to the faculty's assistance. IIT Mandi is surrounded by nature, since it is surrounded by the Uhl River and mountains. As we travel around campus after a grueling day of study in this environment, we gain feeling of relaxation.

Lokendra Singh , IIT Mandi

The first day when I arrived at the campus for the interviews of MS, I was mesmerized by the location of the college. Having done my bachelors from a regional college, IIT Mandi has surely provided more in terms of everything, be it infrastructure, expert faculty, diverse set of people from across the country and even some neighbouring nations. HNT and MTB clubs are one of the best ways of exploring the nature around the campus as well as some parts of Himachal. The clubs/societies at IIT Mandi are a great way of pursuing your interests/hobbies. Hostel life is also a good experience to cherish.Motivation from some of my friends for playing badminton gave me another sense of happiness and I’m enjoying the game a lot.
