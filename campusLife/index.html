Campus Life

IIT Mandi

Campus Infrastructure

IIT Mandi has established modern technological laboratories for B Tech,PG, and PhD students. The technology and equipment are implemented with our student’s needs in mind as they pursue a world-class education supplemented by superior learning methods. All year long, our amphitheatre auditoriums are filled with fresh competitions, celebrations, and activities for our students. The libraries of IIT Mandi, which is located on both the North and South Campuses..., provides books, periodicals, magazines, and other materials to staff and students. There are multiple Reading rooms on campus which provide the perfect environment for our students to delve into their reading and studying, especially during challenging exam times.

Hostels

The cornerstone of our prestigious institution was laid in Kamand, the home of IIT Mandi's permanent campus, on February 24, 2009. Since then it has always been a path to development and achievement of new goals. IIT Mandi is located in the Kamand Valley. The area of ​​IIT Mandi is 538 hectares, of which about 200 hectares are flat land while the rest are mountainous. IIT Campus is spread over North and South campuses in Kamand. North and south campuses are contiguous and interconnected ... with roads and walkways. The innovative avant-garde architecture is designed with landscape volatility in mind. There are separate dormitories for girls and boys with up-to-date maintenance along with a sports room, swimming pool, 24/7 wifi and electricity. We at IIT Mandi are always committed to building sustainable and environmentally friendly initiatives. The Botanical Gardens managed by our experienced and motivated team have housed several species of flora and fauna such as apples, deodars, Himalayan Yew, lychees.

Mess

The mess halls at IIT Mandi offer meals three times a day along with scrumptious snacks in between for the students, staff and faculty who subscribe to the mess facility. There are four mess halls, Pine and Oak at North campus, Cedar and Maple at south campus. The menu is determined by the student mess committee taking into account the nutrient intake of the people at the IIT Mandi. Locally available vegetables not only support our hunger, but also the nearby community.
