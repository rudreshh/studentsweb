Research Scholar Grievance Redressal Committee

The RSGRC is formed for redressing the grievances received from the Research Scholars regarding difficulties faced by them due to interpersonal relations with research and TA supervisors or course instructors.

RSGRC Responsibilites :

  • Committee will address only such grievances of research scholars which relate to their research/TA/project supervisor(s) or course instructor in which he/she is registered and affect their progress in research or coursework.
  • Member Secretary will receive the grievance from M.Sc., M.S., M.Tech., Ph.D., I - Ph.D. students and will initiate the process for its redressal.
  • If any of the committee members is named in the grievance submitted by the student, then a suitable alternate person will be nominated by the Dean (Academics).
  • The committee shall meet as and when required and may invite any other faculty, if required
